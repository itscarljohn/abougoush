Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What should I bring to my free consultation?

No, there is no obligation to hire the lawyer after your consultation. You are free to explore other options if you choose.

3. How long does a free consultation typically last?

Consultations usually last between 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the complexity of your case and the questions you have.

4. Can I get legal help if I'm still employed?

Yes! Many employment lawyers can provide guidance even if you're still working. They can help address issues like workplace harassment or contract disputes proactively.
